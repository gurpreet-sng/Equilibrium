Sport Climbing Courses

Level 1

This course is the introduction to sport climbing.

Once Completed provides an individual with

  • An understanding of all the concepts of climbing (Speed, Lead, Bouldering)
  • Skill sets required to climb (Gear Setup, Top rope climbing, Top rop-1e belaying, Introduction to bouldering)

Level 2

This course take upon the skills learnt in the level 1 course and improves upon it with an experience in the Outdoors.

Once completed an individual should be able to

  • Lead Climb Indoors
  • Lead Belay Indoors
  • Spot indoors
  • Fall properly
